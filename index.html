Projects

Overview

My project is to design an application that can tell if you are intoxicated and locks your phone. The app would take a scan your face and even produce some logical questions that you would have answer correctly within a set amount of time in order to make calls or send messages. Of course, you would still be able to make emergency calls, but certain phone numbers would be blocked. This would include social media apps, to prevent you from posting things that you might regret in the morning.

Motivation

The motivation for an app like this is obvious. Most people have had a moment of weakness while they are out celebrating. We have all heard about people that have decided that after 5+ hours of solid drinking and partying that now would be a good time to call their ex! If not calling/ messaging someone they probably shouldn’t, the other problem is that social media is always open. Today apps like Facebook are so widely used that even prospective employers check your social media pages when you are applying for a job. There is definitely a market for this kind of app, when researching I found that there are over four million results when searching online for this kind of app. While there are some apps already out there for this kind of thing, they are quite new and still have a lot of bugs. Also there are ways to get around the program, these issues are what I am aiming to address.

Project Description

To build this app there would be a need for multiple checks. It would need to have face recognition software incorporated, to stop you from getting someone else to unlock your phone for you. The user would take a photo of themselves before commencing drinking and then one when they are trying to use their phone the software would compare the two photos. Determine whether you were in fact intoxicated and prevent you from using the device until you could show that you were no longer in that state. The program would also randomly generate some logical questions that a person in a good state of mind would be able to answer easily. These questions would have a time limit in which you could answer them. If these questions or the photo check was failed, then you would not be able to try again for one hour.

It would also be linked to your social accounts so assuming these checks were failed your friends would not be able to tag you in their posts. This would require you to link your social accounts to the app, but if you have ever been embarrassed by this kind of behavior then you would not hesitate to sync everything up while you are sober.

As well as comparing your two photos, the facial recognition software would check for certain markers in your facial features. These markers would be pre-programmed into the software from known intoxicated photos. Markers that a police officer or security guard would look for when determining whether you are intoxicated. Once intoxication has been detected in the user, the app could contact someone that you have pre-programmed into the database. Notifying that person, possibly a best friend, that you are trying to access your phone when you probably shouldn’t be. That person can then contact you and stop you from potentially doing something that you would regret in the morning.

You would be able to receive calls and reply to messages, just not initiate the contact. Of course, you would be able to contact emergency services if you needed to even after failing the checks. Two other emergency numbers would be able to be contacted. This would have to be nominated at the beginning of the night while you are coherent. All other contacts would be blocked until the next day or when you can pass the checks again. This would disable the app until you used it the next time. This will help you to keep your dignity in-tact and prevent any unacceptable post/ photos of you being circulated around your social circles and the internet. Keeping your professional life safe from a judgemental emploer all from simply having a fun night out.

Tools

. First thing you would need is an app making tool like Buildfire or the iBuildApp to name a few. There are many free sites on the internet that would allow you to build the basics of an app. This would allow you to build the foundation. However, with the kind of technology needed to make this app work you would need a good programmer to fill in the gaps. Lots of research on the markers that would constitute an intoxicated person and apply them to the photos that are taken. The app would require permissions both from social platforms as well as your phone itself.

Skills Required

As mentioned before, the main skills required for this project would be a very good and accomplished programmer. Someone who knows not only how to build an app, but someone who knows how to work with facial recognition software and manipulate it. Comparing photos to others and to the markers that were mentioned earlier. There would need to be the ability to deploy this program once written. The programmer would be very easy to find, as there are many of great programmers out there many of whom have their own projects that they work on in their spare time.

Outcome

Once successful, this kind of program as an app will be a very powerful tool to those who want to enjoy themselves and feel safe. You will no longer be able to access the contacts that you shouldn't be while you shouldn't be. If you are one of those people who “drunk dial” people, then this will be a great innovation. It has happened to everyone at some point, and this app would stop it from happening. Allowing you to go out and enjoy yourself without fear of repercussions either professionally or personally.
